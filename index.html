Frequently Asked Gutter Cleaning Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things people ask us.

We recommend cleaning your gutters at least twice a year - typically in autumn after leaves fall and in spring before heavy summer rains. Properties with lots of trees nearby may need more frequent cleaning, up to 3-4 times per year.

We service the entire Hunter Valley region including Maitland, Newcastle, Cessnock, Lake Macquarie, Port Stephens, and Singleton. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just give us a call or submit a quote request.

In most cases, no. Our ground-based vacuum system can reach most residential gutters without needing roof access. For unusual roof designs or extreme blockages, we may need brief roof access, but we always discuss this with you first and take all precautions.

A typical single-storey home takes 45 minutes to 1.5 hours depending on the amount of debris. Double-storey homes may take 1-2 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when we provide your quote.

All debris is vacuumed directly into our collection tank on the truck. We take it away with us, so there's no mess left on your property. Your gutters and yard will be cleaner than when we arrived.
